Biography

KeyTonic, born Carlo Denti on March 27, 2010, is a young and rising experimental rock and hip-hop artist. He started his musical journey at the age of 5 when he began playing piano, quickly showcasing his gift for perfect pitch. By 2021, at just 11 years old, Carlo began producing and releasing his own music under the name KeyTonic, focusing on blending elements of rock and hip-hop with a unique, experimental twist. With an unrelenting drive and talent beyond his years, KeyTonic is a name to watch out for in the music world.
